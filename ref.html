Use the Fridge as a Bookcase

It is not a big deal to turn an old, unused fridge into a bookcase. Yes, anyone can do it! this is a fine idea to repurpose old refrigerator into a storage unit that will assist in banishing clutter from your home. Such a repurposed fridge bookcase can be ideally placed in a study or elsewhere.Try and keep the original look of the fridge, that is the key to an interesting make. Use stickers and small toys to enhance the appeal, if you want to.
