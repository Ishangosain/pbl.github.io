Use CDs as lamp

1)Gathering materials:-We need six CDs (or just a CD box ), a soldering iron and a small lamp that will fit in our CD box. The lamp have to be not too small, because it would go out of the CD box.

2)Making of whole thing:- A. If you don't have a CD box The construction is very easy. You have to place the cd's at 90 degree angle, and then you have to make little holes between them using hot soldering iron. Now if the "standing" cd won't fall, it means you can add third one (just the same way you attached the previous two CD's to each other). If it looks like the (3 CDs) construction on photo, repeat what whole thing to have two CD corners(made of three CDs). Now we have to put the lamp between those two corners, and then connect corners just to make a CD box.

B. If you do have a CD box then we have to unattach one CD of the box, put the lamp inside and then use soldering iron to put the CD back to its place.

3) Light your room with your cd lamp:- Now you can light the room with your lamp. It looks kickass especially in night.I hope you enjoyed my instructable. Caution:- Keep in your mind that the lamp can melt CDs from the heat generated by lamp.
